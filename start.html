GAME

This audio quiz game is thought as an interactive tool to train your recognition skills for calls and songs of some of the birds you could encounter on the island.

Every round will be completed after 5 answered cards.

You can skip up to two cards that won't be considered on the total score!

If you find it hard to identify the species only by its sound you can look at the image, but once you do you will only get half of the score for the respective card.

Points: The maximum score is given for the scientific name of every species. The italian or english common names are almost as good though. If you're not sure about the species you can also try your best typing in a genus or a family, but this will give significantly lower scores!

Note that, while capitalization doesn't play a role for an answer to be accepted, excessive spaces can indeed mess with your score!


Have fun learning!
