This page is the brainchild of two young outdoor enthusiasts from Sicily, one of the richest regions in Europe in terms of biodiversity.

One of the things that we enjoy most during nature walks is trying to spot and identify all the birds we meet along the way. The diversity of the sicilian avifauna is immense, especially during migration periods, making the game quite compelling.

One of the most powerful elements for identifying bird species is undoubtedly the sound they make. Very often, in fact, birds are very difficult to see, while hearing them is much easier.

Identifying a species by its sound, however, is not always so easy, especially for amateur birdwatchers like us. Therefore we thought of creating a little game to train ourselves to recognise the multitude of songs and calls out there, and here we want to share it with all those who share our interest :)

Sure, it's getting easier and easier nowadays to identify a species with the help of a smartphone - but where's the pleasure in that?

If you too would like to test or improve your bird recognition skills, give our game a try!
