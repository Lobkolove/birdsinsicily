SEXISM DISCLAIMER

Note that many species display a sexual dimorphism, meaning that the looks of male and female individuals can vary from one another (quite a lot in some cases).
Many pictures shown on this page are depicting only a male individual. The reason for this is that males often display more distinct morphological traits than females.
We are working to add pictures of both sexes for all birds displaying sexual dimorphism though!
